# Article screenshots

Drop your three news article screenshots here as:

- `article-1.png` — first headline (segment 01-headline-1, 0:00 – 0:10)
- `article-2.png` — second headline (segment 02-headline-2, 0:10 – 0:20)
- `article-3.png` — third headline (segment 03-headline-3, 0:20 – 0:33)

Recommended:

- 16:10 or 16:9 ratio so the rounded card frame doesn't crop awkwardly
- ≥ 1500 px wide for crisp 1080p / 4K renders
- PNG or high-quality JPG; the file extension just needs to match the
  reference in `src/segments.ts`

Until the files are present, the article slots render an empty card; the
voiceover and captions still play.
