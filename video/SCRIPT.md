# Primum — demo script

**Total runtime: 2:13.5 · 18 segments · 30 fps · paper-light theme**

The spine is conceptual. Each platform step has a synthetic slide that
explains *what* it does and *why* it matters, followed by a real-app
screenshot as evidence ("see, it ships"). Silent screenshot beats hold
the visual after each narration so the eye can finish reading the page.

## Tone direction (for the voiceover model)

| Section | Tone | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 01–03 (articles) | hushed → firm | Open quiet. The third headline lands the diagnosis. |
| 04 (stakes) | firm | Statement, not argument. |
| 05–08 (Apollo / triage / bulk) | neutral | Demo voice. Each verb does work. |
| 08b–10 (bench / queues / signoff / training) | neutral | Workflow narration; the screenshots carry the proof. |
| 11 (synthesis) | neutral | One synthesis sentence — let the visual breathe. |
| 12 (impact) | warm | Land the medical-sector promise without selling. |

## Pause discipline

- 300–500 ms breaks at clause boundaries — clinical writing breathes.
- 500–700 ms on the close so the motto doesn't sprint.
- No music bed. The pauses *are* the bed.

## Cited instruments (visible in the app, named in the narration)

- **CAPE-II** — Community Assessment of Psychic Experiences
- **C-SSRS** — Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale
- **Joiner framework** — Joiner's interpersonal-psychological theory of suicide / acquired-capability marker
- **DSM-5-TR**, **APA Ethics 3.05 / 9.01**, **MITI 4.2.1** — surfaced in post-mortem rationale chips
- **988 Lifeline** — escalation referral
